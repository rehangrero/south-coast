Payment Confirmation & Activation

8.1 Client Action

Client must:

  • Email payment confirmation OR
  • Send payment proof via WhatsApp
Exception â€“ Online Payments:
If the client makes a direct online card payment, no manual update is required in the eTenders Admin Panel, as:
  • Payment status
  • Subscription details
are automatically updated in the Admin system.
8.2 Record Payment in Xero (Mandatory for All Payments)
Log in to Xero
Open the issued invoice
Record payment:
  • Mark invoice as Paid
  • Ensure payment date matches the actual funds received date
Confirm invoice balance is zero
8.3 Activate Subscription in eTenders Admin (Only for Non-Online Payments)
Log in to eTenders Admin Panel
Open client profile
Activate subscription:
  • Select correct plan
  • Set subscription end date: 6 months / 12 months / 24 months from payment received date
Confirm expiry date auto-calculates correctly
Ensure platform access is enabled
Note: Skip this step if payment was made online and auto-activated.
